Spicy Chicken Recipe

This delicious, thick stew has a spicy kick infused with rich chicken parts to create a hearty meal.

Need to cook a quick, hearty meal under 25 minutes, then this is the recipe for you. Not only does it provide a ton of hearty vegetables and proteins, but it also packs quite a kick! You can alter the spiciness of the stew, but it's recommend to use the recommended portions for full flavor and consistency.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 to 3 pounds of bone-in chicken parts cut into small pieces
  • 10 ounces of potatoes cut into chunks
  • 1 medium carrot cut into chunks
  • 1/2 large onion cut into chunks
  • 2-3 thinly sliced ginger pieces

Sauce

  • 2-3 tablespoons gochugaru
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 4 tablespoons of mirin
  • 2 tablespoons of gochujang
  • 1 pinch of pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of seasame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Prepare vegetables by cutting them into chunks
  2. Add 1.5 cups of water to a large pot along with the chicken and the sauce (except the seasame oil and sesame seeds). Stir well and bring to boil over a high heat. Reduce heat to medium after boiling and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in vegetables. Cover and cook for 15 minutes, stirring sporadically.
  4. Cook uncovered and wait for sauce to thicken after 5 minutes. Add in sesame oil and sesame seeds.
